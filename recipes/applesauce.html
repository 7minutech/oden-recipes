Applesauce

Description

A quick and easy applesauce recipe that is so good you got to have it on the ready

Ingredients

  • 6 cups apples - peeled, cored and chopped
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Over medium heat combine apples,water, cinnamon, and cloves to a 2 quart saucepan
  2. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 10 minutes
  3. Stir in sugar, and simmer 5 more minutes
